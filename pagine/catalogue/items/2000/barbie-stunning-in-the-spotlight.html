Barbie® Stunning in the Spotlight™

Character Barbie
Series Barbie Fashion Model Collection
Released date 2009
Designer Robert Best
Label Gold label
MPN N6603

Barbie stunning in the Spotlight™ celebrates 10 years of the Barbie Fashion Models Collection.
She is a gold label doll with no more than 7000 copies worldwide, her pricetag at release was 161,69$. She is no longer available on the Mattel site so it must be bought via a third party.

This series of beautifully crafted dolls, designed from 2000 to 2020 by Robert Best, are made of Silkstone, a type of vinyl that has the look, weight, and feel of porcelain.

The doll also commemorates the 50th anniversary of Barbie by updating the iconic 1960 realease Solo in the Spotlight™. The doll pays hommage to the original by giving it a modern twist with a full lenght mermaid gown of black chiffon, with a pink eye-catching stole. The dress is covered with black rhinestones embelishement. She wears dramatic dangling earings, long black gloves and comes of course witj a silvery vintage style microphone and a stand for collectors.

The back of the box reads: Barbie Fashion Model Collection Stunning in the spotlight. Since 1959" Collection commemorates famed vintage fashions, re-invented for a new era!
A dusky stage, a single spotlight, a sultry solo: Barbie® doll is always the dramatic, superlative star! 1960s Solo in the Spotlight™ fashion inspires a stylish reinvention from Robert Best in celebration of Barbie doll's 50th anniversary.
