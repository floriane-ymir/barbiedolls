Barbiedollz is an archive rich in descrition, photographs and videos relating to Barbie fashion line dolls.
The site aim is to preserve in an organized and logical system the rich catalogue of the mattel's line.

The dolls don't come up with just a descrition of the characteristic of the toy, but also the story behind such dresses enriched with details about the designers that made this magic possible.

With the patterns and video tutorials of the "dress up like barbie" portal, you also can replicate the amazing garments, be it for another doll-scale project, or up-scale them to fit human proportions.
